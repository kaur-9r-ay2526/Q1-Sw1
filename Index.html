My Top 3 Favorite Shows!


1. Never Have I Ever(2020)


The series "Never Have I Ever" narrates the life of an Indian girl in America who battles her own life traumas all while trying to be "popular" in school, keep up her image as valedictorian, and stand up to her mother's never ending expectations. I often find myself resonating and relating to Devi as I see her in myself, since I am also a foreigner just trying to get by. These relating aspects are some of the reasons why this show is my Top 1.

2. Ginny and Georgia(2021)


Ginny and Georgia follows the life of a teenage girl living with her single mom who has too many secrets and her little brother who is just like his sister. She tries to keep up her relationships and friendships in her new highschool as her Mom's horrific past slowly unfolds and ruins her life. This show is really entertaining all in all and has many plot twists which is why I find myself always rewatching it.

3. Alexa and Katie(2018)


Alexa and Katia is a show about two bestfriends who go through everything together and stay by eachother especially when one of them is diagnosed with Cancer. Even after Alexa beats Cancer, Katie stays by her side through everything and the main plot is if they can really stay best friends through everything. I enjoy this series as it often reminds of the true value of friendship.
